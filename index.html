60-30-10-Rule

The 60-30-10 rule emphasizes key design elements by allocating percentages to dominant, secondary, and accent colors, creating visual balance and simplicity. Dominant color sets the mood, secondary color supports it, and the 10% accent color highlights specific features.

Typography

Typography defines hierarchy

Typography is an essential factor in establishing hierarchy within design. Variable font weights are used to convey varying levels of importance, with the heavier weights indicating a higher degree of significance.

Nunito Sans

Nunito, a versatile sans-serif typeface, forms the core of our design system, emphasizing simplicity and consistency. It encompasses two versions: Nunito with rounded terminals and Nunito Sans with non-rounded terminals. We use Nunito exclusively to ensure a unified design approach.
